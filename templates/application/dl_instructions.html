INSTRUCTIONS

  1.  Applicants from New South Wales will apply only at Consulate General of Pakistan Sydney for their driving license verification.
  2.  In case of Pakistani Driving License, it should first be verified from the license issuing authority in Pakistan by obtaining a NOC attested from the Ministry of  Foreign Affairs in Islamabad or its Camp Offices at Karachi, Lahore, Peshawar and Quetta.
  3.  If the driving license is verifiable online from the official database, requirement of obtaining NOC can be waived off.
  4.  Applicants desiring to have letter of authenticity of the license or requesting confirmation of name as appearing in license etc must also fill the statuary  declaration signed by Notary Public or J.P. and submit the same to the Consulate General in original. This declaration would be enclosed in the letter addressed to the relevant RTA.
  5.  This form can either be submitted personally or through register / courier mail.
  6.  Applicant is required to submit or send a self addressed, Express or Registered Post return envelope to receive outcome by post.
  7.  The validity of Driving License Verification remains one year. After one year, you need to re-apply to have a fresh verification.
  8.  Submitting fake documents, including fraudulent attestations, is a felony under Commonwealth Law. Fake documents with the application will be reported  to Roads and Maritime and Police. which may prohibit any future chance of obtaining a driving license.
